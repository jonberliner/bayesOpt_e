Instructions

At each drilling site, along with the samples you already see, you will get to choose one more location to sample.

You only get one extra sample, and it will cost a bit of money to use, so be sure use this sample where ever you think it will help you most!

To sample, click the ground line (the brighter brown line) where you would like to know how deep it is. You will know you can click because the ground will glow a small amount when you place your mouse over it.
