Instructions

To sample, you must click on the ground line where ever you think it will best help you find as deep an area as possible.

To sample mineral line depth at a location, click the ground (tan stripe)

You can tell you can click becuse the ground will glow when you hover your mouse over it.

Note that it will cost a significant amount of money to drill at each location once the crew arrives, so be sure to choose where to sample wisely!
