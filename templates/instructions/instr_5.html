Instructions

After a few samples, your mining crew will arrive and drill at the location of the deepest mineral deposit that you have discovered.

You will not know exactly when your crew arrives, so you want to try and find as deep an area as you can as fast as you can.

The deepest spot you have found determines how much money you will earn in that area.

A red marker will surround the drilled location showing how deep your crew drilled in this area.

The mineral deposit line will appear as well, showing you how deep the minerals were in this area.

A message will also appear showing you how much you earned from drilling in this area, after deducting drilling costs.
